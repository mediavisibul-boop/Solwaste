## Content Additions from Article

### Who Are Large Waste Generators?
According to India's Solid Waste Management Rules, bulk or large waste generators include:
- Hotels, resorts & restaurants
- Malls & commercial complexes
- IT parks & corporate campuses
- Hospitals & healthcare facilities
- Educational institutions
- Food courts & food processing units

**Legal Requirement:** Facilities generating more than 100 kg of organic waste per day must manage waste at source.

### Why Composting is Critical for Large Generators
1. Rising waste disposal costs
2. Strict municipal inspections
3. Risk of fines & penalties
4. Odour, pest & hygiene issues
5. Negative ESG & sustainability impact

### ROI & Cost Benefits
- Waste hauling costs drop significantly
- Payback period often under 24 months
- Compost can be reused or sold
- Avoid municipal penalties
- Pass compliance inspections easily
